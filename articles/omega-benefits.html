The Benefits of Omega-3

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential nutrients that play a crucial role in your overall health. Found in high concentrations in certain fish, algae, and seal oil, these healthy fats have been linked to numerous physical and cognitive benefits.

1. Supports Heart Health

Omega-3s help reduce triglyceride levels, lower blood pressure, and prevent plaque buildup in arteries — all of which contribute to a healthier heart.

2. Boosts Brain Function

DHA, a type of Omega-3, is a major structural component of the brain. It supports memory, focus, and may help reduce age-related cognitive decline.

3. Reduces Inflammation

Omega-3s have anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce symptoms of arthritis and improve joint mobility and comfort.

4. Supports Eye Health

DHA is also a key nutrient for retina function, which can help prevent macular degeneration and maintain good vision.

5. Promotes Overall Well-being

Studies suggest Omega-3s may improve mood, reduce symptoms of depression, and support a stronger immune system.
