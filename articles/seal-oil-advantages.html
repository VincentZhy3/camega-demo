The Unique Advantages of Seal Oil: Nature's Most Complete Omega-3 Source

Discover why seal oil offers superior benefits compared to traditional fish oil supplements.

While fish oil has long been the standard source of Omega-3 fatty acids, seal oil offers unique advantages that make it a superior choice for optimal health. Let's explore why seal oil stands out in the world of Omega-3 supplements.

Complete Omega-3 Profile

Seal oil contains a unique combination of three essential Omega-3 fatty acids:

  • DHA (Docosahexaenoic Acid): Essential for brain and eye health
  • EPA (Eicosapentaenoic Acid): Supports heart health and inflammation response
  • DPA (Docosapentaenoic Acid): A rare Omega-3 found only in seal oil, enhancing the benefits of DHA and EPA

Superior Absorption

Seal oil offers better bioavailability compared to fish oil due to several factors:

  • Natural triglyceride form that's easier for the body to absorb
  • Unique molecular structure that enhances uptake
  • Higher concentration of active Omega-3s per serving
  • Better retention in cell membranes

Enhanced Health Benefits

The complete Omega-3 profile in seal oil provides comprehensive health support:

  • Improved cognitive function and brain health
  • Better cardiovascular support
  • Enhanced joint health and mobility
  • Stronger immune system function

Sustainable and Ethical Sourcing

Our seal oil is sourced with the highest standards of sustainability and ethics:

  • Harvested from sustainable populations in the Canadian North
  • Supports Indigenous communities and traditional practices
  • Minimal environmental impact
  • Strict quality control and traceability
